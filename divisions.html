Operational Divisions of FCSF

  • Cyber Threat Intelligence Division (CTID): Gathers and analyzes intelligence on emerging cyber threats, including nation-state actors, criminal organizations, and zero-day vulnerabilities.
  • Incident Response & Crisis Management Division (IRCMD): Provides 24/7 response to cyberattacks, coordinating across government, private sector, and international partners to contain and mitigate threats.
  • Digital Forensics & Investigations Division (DFID): Conducts in-depth forensic analysis, preserving digital evidence for prosecution and intelligence exploitation.
  • Critical Infrastructure Defense Division (CIDD): Monitors, audits, and secures vital national assets including energy, defense, telecommunications, and finance systems.
  • Policy, Strategy & Legal Affairs Division (PSLAD): Develops national cyber defense policies, ensures legal compliance, and advises on cross-border cybersecurity law and diplomacy.
  • Cybersecurity Training & Capacity Building Division (CTCBD): Conducts internal training, public awareness campaigns, and provides technical guidance to national institutions.
