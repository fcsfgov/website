Guardians of the Digital Frontier

The Federal Cyber Security Force (FCSF) is Pakistan’s lead cyber defense agency, established to protect the nation’s critical digital infrastructure from modern cyber warfare, espionage, and sabotage. Our mandate is to secure, defend, and advance the country’s digital interests at all levels—government, military, and public sector.

Our Mission

We work around the clock to identify threats, neutralize hostile actors, and build resilience within our national networks. Combining technological precision with strategic intelligence, FCSF serves as the backbone of Pakistan’s digital security.

Collaboration, innovation, and vigilance define our approach as we lead the frontline in cyberspace defense.

Threat Monitoring

Constant surveillance of national digital assets to detect intrusion, data breaches, and suspicious activity in real-time.

Rapid Response

Deployment-ready task forces and digital responders equipped to isolate, counter, and mitigate hostile cyber incidents 24/7.

Digital Forensics

Advanced forensic capabilities for tracking, analyzing, and legally documenting cyber intrusions and violations.
