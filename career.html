Careers at FCSF

The Federal Cyber Security Force (FCSF) offers opportunities for dedicated individuals to begin their journey in national cybersecurity. Our selection process emphasizes integrity, adaptability, and discretion.

All candidates are initially considered for the role of Cyber Trainee. Upon assessment of performance, aptitude, and operational conduct, promotions and specialized assignments may be offered.

Details regarding roles, evaluations, and career progression are confidential and shared only with successful applicants.
