About the FCSF

The Federal Cyber Security Force (FCSF) is Pakistan's premier cyber defense agency operating under the national cyber command. Founded in response to increasing cyber threats targeting national sovereignty, the FCSF’s mission is to ensure the integrity, availability, and confidentiality of Pakistan’s critical information infrastructure.

With a highly specialized and vetted team, the FCSF functions through intelligence-driven operations, advanced monitoring technologies, and rigorous enforcement of cybersecurity protocols. Our analysts and field officers work around the clock to detect, disrupt, and dismantle cyber espionage, sabotage, and criminal activity targeting the homeland.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Monitoring and responding to advanced persistent threats (APTs)
  • Safeguarding governmental, defense, and critical infrastructure networks
  • Conducting proactive threat hunting and forensic investigations
  • Collaborating with allied national and international cyber defense organizations
  • Training public sector entities on cyber hygiene and incident readiness

The FCSF is not just a force; it is the shield of the nation's digital frontier. We welcome partnership and vigilance from all sectors to protect what matters most.
